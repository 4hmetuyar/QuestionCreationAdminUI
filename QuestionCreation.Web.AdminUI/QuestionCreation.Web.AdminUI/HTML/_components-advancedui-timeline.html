Simple Feed

Notification Feed

If timelines dont suit your needs, you can use de the simple activity feed to display notifications or whatever chronological content.

  1. 2 minutes ago Henry Rodstein has marked 4 invoices as processed and paid.
  2. 3 hours ago Marjory Cambell has locked and archived 2 invoices
  3. 5 hours ago Anthony Kowalsky created 2 new opportunities
  4. Yesterday Kareem Jabbar has marked 3 invoices with issues and set their priority to urgent.
  5. Monday Eleanor Briggs uploaded 5 new invoices.
  6. Last week Kevin Smith has marked 9 invoices as processed and paid.

Activity Feed

Avatars can also easily replaced by icons to emphasize your feed content design.

  1. 2 minutes ago Henry Rodstein sent you a Message.
  2. 3 hours ago You have 5 new messages in your inbox.
  3. 5 hours ago 3 more users are now following you.
  4. Yesterday You have 3 overdue invoices pending.
  5. Monday Eleanor Briggs liked your Publication.
  6. Last week Kevin Smith added a new Folder to an ongoing Project.
